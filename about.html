CBI POlytechnic

Centre for Bioinformatics was established under the Trust SNEH (Society for Nature, Education and Health) in 2003, registered under Indian Trust Act, Regd. No. IV/ 1413, as a non-profit organization. The institute was established as a premier Biological, Medical, Paramedical and Technological Institution. with the aim of providing high quality education in Engineering and Technology to the students of the country, especially in the State as also for seeking potential employment outside the State The foundation aims to be the best education hub in the region It is a self-supporting, self-financed and privately managed institute. CBI is situated on the Tupudana ,Hatia national highway about 5 km from nearest Railway station. Academic knowledge is imparted by highly qualified and eminent teachers with the active support of other trained staff exposing the students to current industrial aspects through Industry-Institute interaction at appropriate levels.

VISION OF CENTRE FOR BIOINFORMATICS

 The vision of the college is to empower the under privileged and downtrodden people of the society specially ST, SC and OBC and PH. The college endeavours to instill the quality of creativity, innovations and benevolence inspiring the students fraternity to strive for equity and social justice. Our college aims at the integral formation of prospective teachers for the establishment of a just and humane society. We visualize the overall development of would be teachers who will be the storehouse of knowledge and with this potential knowledge, they will have the unlimited abilities and capacities to spread the wisdom all around.
