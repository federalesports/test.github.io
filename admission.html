HOW TO GET ADMISSION

Admission in Diploma Engineering courses is done through the entrance examinations conducted by JCECEB, Govt. of Jharkhand. Candidates who qualify in the said examinations (Common Merit Rank) are eligible to take admission in the respective courses. Admission at the Institute level, in case of vacant seats, shall be done on merit, and shall be taken after the counseling process of JCECEB is over. Candidates outside the state of Jharkhand shall also be eligible for admission.



Eligibility Criteria

FIRST YEAR

A candidate should have obtained at least 35% in 10th Std/Matric/SSC examination.

DIRECT SECOND YEAR

A candidate should be 12th Science (with Mathematics as one of the subject) OR 10th + (2 years ITI) with appropriate specialization OR 12th Science with Vocational / Technical.
